Daily rhythm

Build awareness through simple morning and evening reflection.

Upp keeps check-ins short and repeatable. Morning entries focus on how you feel, what you appreciate, and what matters today. Evening entries help you reflect on what went well, what felt hard, and what deserves gratitude.

  • Short prompts that fit into real life
  • Gentle reminders that support routine without guilt
  • Structured reflection instead of overwhelming journaling

Calendar history

Notice how your days begin, end, and evolve.

Browse past entries by day, week, month, or year in a lightweight calendar view that feels native to iPhone. The current day stands out clearly, and each date can quietly show whether you checked in once or twice.

  • Move back through time without losing the thread
  • See morning and evening entry indicators at a glance
  • Review emotional context without digging through long notes
Weekly insights

See the emotional patterns behind your weeks.

Upp turns your check-ins into gentle reflections about streaks, themes, and shifts in how the week felt. The tone stays human and observational, helping you understand yourself without sounding clinical or mechanical.

  • Narrative observations shaped by mood and journal history
  • Simple trend views instead of stats-heavy dashboards
  • Streaks that encourage consistency, not pressure
